Color Palette

Primary Palette

Primary brand colors are used for elements that reflect Happening's brand. Each color has a lighter, darker, and muted shade.

Secondary Palette

Secondary brand colors contain a variety of colors to keep things fresh and interesting. They consist of additional variations of the existing primary brand colors, including true, extra light, extra dark, deep muted, light muted, and extra light muted shades. When used in conjunction with our primary palette, these colors capture the nuance of each experience.
